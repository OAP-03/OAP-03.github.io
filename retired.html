Retired from Sad, New Career in Business

Released on August 1, 2013

Main Page

Overview

This is Mitski's second album, released in 2013 and features nine tracks. The person on the cover is not Mitski.

The Tracks:

  1. Goodbye, My Danish Sweetheart
  2. Square
  3. Strawberry Blond
  4. Humpty
  5. I Want You
  6. Shame
  7. Because Dreaming Costs Money, My Dear
  8. Circle
  9. Class of 2013
Extra Tracks!
  1. Square (Solo Piano Version)
  2. Shame (Jammin' Out Solo Version)

My Review

  • Retired from Sad, New Career in Business also has that emotional rawness that Lush has. The album mainly features orchestral music, as Mitski used the student orchestra at her college.
  • Some tracks on this album are hard to listen to for newer fans. I used to dislike many of the songs on this album, but now I like most of them. One of the songs, "Strawberry Blonde," is very popular among TikTok, as listeners took it as a cottagecore sound (even though it is about POC struggles). My favorite tracks on this album are "I Want You" and "Humpty."
