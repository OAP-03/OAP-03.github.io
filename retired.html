Retired from Sad, New Career in Business

Released on August 1, 2013

Main Page

Overview

This is Mitski's third album, which she wrote when she was exhausted from having to make money after college. She learned guitar for it, and wrote the whole album by herself. The album features ten tracks, and was released in 2014.

The Tracks:

  1. Goodbye, My Danish Sweetheart
  2. Square
  3. Strawberry Blond
  4. Humpty
  5. I Want You
  6. Shame
  7. Because Dreaming Costs Money, My Dear
  8. Circle
  9. Class of 2013
