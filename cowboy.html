Be the Cowboy

Released on August 17, 2018

Main Page

Overview

Be the Cowboy is Mitski's fifth album. She widens the instruments she uses, bringing back her piano, while adding synthesizers, horns, and her guitar. It contains fourteen tracks, and was released in 2018.

The Tracks:

  1. Geyser
  2. Why Didn't You Stop Me?
  3. Old Friend
  4. A Pearl
  5. Lonesome Love
  6. Remember My Name
  7. Me and My Husband
  8. Come into the Water
  9. Nobody
  10. Pink in the Night
  11. A Horse Named Cold Air
  12. Washing Machine Heart
  13. Blue Light
  14. Two Slow Dancers

My Review

  • This album's title was inspired by a peer from college who performed with "cowboy swagger." After she graduated, she missed seeing him perform and became the cowboy. This album is about changing identity, by using accommodation, self-advancement, and self-actualization.
  • Be the Cowboy is one of her best albums! It was released before her four-year break. I love this album because its music is amazing. The tracks have a wide range of instruments and emotions behind them. My favorite songs on this album are "A Pearl" and "Washing Machine Heart."
