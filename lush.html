Lush

Created on January 31, 2012

Main Page

Overview

Lush is Mitski's first album, which she wrote as her junior year project for university. It was self-released in 2012, and has nine tracks.

The Tracks:

  1. Liquid Smooth
  2. Wife
  3. Abbey
  4. Brand New City
  5. Eric
  6. Bag of Bones
  7. Door
  8. Pearl Diver
  9. Real Men

My Review

  • Lush is one of Mitski's more emotional albums. It is also more musically diverse than her other albums, as she wrote the songs at different periods in her life. The first song she wrote was "Bag of Bones" when she was eighteen and living in Turkey.
  • This album is one of my favorites because of its range of emotions. I find it easy to get lost in the music, as Mitski's vocals can sway you to feel what Mitski feels. My favorite song on this album is "Liquid Smooth" or "Door."
